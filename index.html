The Bear, FX

What is 'The Bear'?

The Bear is about Carmy, a professional Chef working in New York City, when he gets a call that his older brother has commited suicde and left the family resturant, The Bear, under his name. He returns back home to Chicago to take hold of the resturant and work to bring it back to it's glory, so he can pay off it's debt.. which his brother also left for him. The resturant comes with staff, some of which are very stubborn, and some family friends who come to help out from time to time, talking about the Fak Brothers. It's ultimately a story about teamwork and family, while also talkign about mental health and purpose of life and why his brother did what he did.

Baby Reindeer, Netflix

What is 'Baby Reindeer'?

This story takes place in London, Egland, more specifically at a pub, where a middle aged, plus size, woman walks in. She sits down at the bar, trying her hardest to hold back her tears, and has no intention of order anything. Its roughly noon, but its London so everyone's drinking all the time. The main character, Donny, a bartender at the pub and aspiring comedic, asks her if she would like to order anything, she quitely shakes her head. "Not even a cup of tea?" asks Donny. "I can't afford it", Martha replies back. Donny felt some pity towards her so he gave her a cup on the house. Martha acknowldges the kind gester and immediately clinges on to Donny. She tells him she's a lawyer and she has worked with many famous clients, and all Donny could think of was "Then why can't she afford a cup of tea?" The first episode wastes no time in hooking us in. We start to realize this is a comedy about a mentally sick stalker who has taken a liking to Donny. But the show takes a sharp turn in the middle and becomes a story about trauma and learning how to cope with it.
